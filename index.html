Our Story

Crafted with Passion

SR Bakes was born from a deep love for the art of chocolate-making and the joy of gifting. Every creation that leaves our kitchen carries with it a promise — of luxury, quality, and unforgettable flavour.

From our signature ruby-pink heart chocolates to intricately designed artisan bars and silky dessert cups, each product is handcrafted using premium ingredients and techniques perfected over years of passionate baking.

We believe every occasion deserves something truly special. Whether it's a celebration, a heartfelt gift, or simply an indulgent moment for yourself — SR Bakes is here to make it extraordinary.
